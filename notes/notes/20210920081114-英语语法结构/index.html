英语语法结构

2021-09-20 :: [Gray King]

双宾语⌗

  • 例句：I want to buy a birthday gift for my sister.
  • 结构：buy sth. for sb.
  • 双宾语：by sb. sth.
    • buy my sister a birthday gift.
    • Can I buy a drink for you? Can I buy you a drink?

双宾语限制⌗

第二个宾语必须为名词，不能是人称代词（pron.）。

下面语句不能使用双宾语

I want to buy you it.(X)

双宾语动词⌗

  • Bring it to me Bring me a present.
  • Give it to me Give me the pen.
  • tell the story to me tell me the story
  • email the photo to me email me the photo
  • sell the handbag to her sell her the handbag
  • take the coffee to her: 不能使用
  • Give me the hand：习语不能使用

宾语从句⌗

在「一句简单句有且只有一个谓语动词」的基础上实现复杂句。

  • I think there is one on Green Street.
    • 主句：I think…
    • 从句：there is…

宾语从句：从句作为宾语。

  • I think (that) there is…
    • that 作为引导标志可以省略
