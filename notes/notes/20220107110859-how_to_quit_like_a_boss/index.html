How to quit like a boss

2022-01-07 :: [Gray King]
  • tags: Career
  • source: “How to Quit like a Boss.” Accessed January 7, 2022. https://jmsbrdy.com/blog/leaving-spring/.
  • HN: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=29830296

From the article:

  • Avoid communication failures: your manager should not be surprised by your leaving.

    • Do have regular, clear, frank conversations about your career with your direct manager.
    • Do tell your manager clearly if there’s something you’re looking for in your career which your current role isn’t providing.
    • Don’t withhold concerns or aspirations from your manager.

    But how can I avoid afraid of leaving?

  • Match your notice period to the handover period

    • Avoid giving to much notice
