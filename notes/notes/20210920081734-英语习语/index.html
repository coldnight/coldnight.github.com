英语习语

2021-09-20 :: [Gray King]

Give me a hand⌗

come in 有⌗

  • come in all/different colors/size

come on 「随意」鼓动、鼓励、催促⌗

Excuse me 抱歉/引起注意⌗

by the way/BTW 顺便说一下⌗

shame on sb.! sb. 可耻⌗

make it 成功达成⌗

do/try one’s best (to do sth.) 尽最大努力做某事⌗

sure thing⌗

no problem.

礼貌请求⌗

礼貌程度⌗

  • please > please
  • 疑问句 > 肯定句
  • could > would > can

示例：

  1. Show me (please)
  2. Can you show me?
  3. Can you show me please?
  4. Would you show me (please)?
  5. Could you show me (please)?

适用场景

  • 随意聊天 1-2
  • 服务场合 3-5
  • 商务场合 4-5

Give me a hand⌗

  1. 别愣着了 Give me a hand (please)!
  2. 正常请求 Can you give me a hand (please)!
  3. 客气请求 Would/Could you give me a hand (please)!

Would like 想要⌗

would like (sb.) to do

身体健康程度⌗

find < good < great

名字和人称顺序⌗

  • Lisa and I I 置后
  • You and Lisa You 置前
