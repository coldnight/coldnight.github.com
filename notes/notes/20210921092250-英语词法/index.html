英语词法

2021-09-21 :: [Gray King]

-ed adj vs -ing adj.⌗

  • -ed adj: sb. 感觉如何
  • -ing adj. sth. 如何

interested / interesting⌗

  • Are you interested?

excited / exciting⌗

  • The TV series is so exciting

比较级⌗

形容词/副词比较级⌗

  1. 常规单音节词 -er
    • fast -> faster
    • small -> smaller
    • nice -> nicer
    • large -> larger（删除词尾不发音的 e）
  2. -y -> -ier
    • busy -> busier
    • pretty -> prettier
  3. 短元音 + 辅音：重写辅音 -er
    • big -> bigger
    • hot -> hotter
  4. 多音节： more +
    • diffcult -> more difficult
    • interesting -> more interesting
    • careful /kɛəful/ -> more careful
  5. -y 二音节词（-ly副词除外）：常不加 more
    • busy -> busier
    • pretty -> prettier
    • quickly -> more quickly
  6. 特殊
    • much/many -> more
    • little -> less
    • good/well -> better
    • bad -> worse

代词比较级⌗

  • more
  • less

比较级修饰⌗

  • a little/ a bit + 比较级 更…一点
  • much / a lot / far + 比较级 更…得多

open/close⌗

open⌗

  1. When do you open(v.)? 强调动态，时间点
    • We are open at 9 every day.
  2. When are you open(adj.)? 强调静态，时间段
    • We are open from 9 to 6 every day.

close⌗

  • close v. 动态
  • closed adj. 静态

示例

  1. When do you close?
    • We close at 12 am.
  2. When are you closed?
    • We are closed from 10 pm to 10 am.

each/every⌗

  • each 强调个体
  • every 强调整体都

都加单数

co- 前缀：联合、共同⌗

  • co-writer
  • co-worker
