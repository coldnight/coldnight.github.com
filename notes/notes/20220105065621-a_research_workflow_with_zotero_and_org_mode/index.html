A research workflow with Zotero and Org mode

2022-01-05 :: [Gray King]
  • tags: Org Mode,Taking Notes,Zotero,Research,Emacs
  • source: “A Research Workflow with Zotero and Org Mode | Mkbehr.Com.” Accessed January 5, 2022. http://www.mkbehr.com/posts/a-research-workflow-with-zotero-and-org-mode/.

Gluing zotero and Org mode together with zotxt(zotxt-emacs).

Workflow:

  1. Store papers into zotero by its browser plugin, that may also download the PDF.
  2. Create a page in Emacs and link to zotero via zotxt-emacs C-c " ".
  3. When I want to read the paper. Go to the page in Emacs and type C-c " a.
  4. When I’m reading a paper and see a citation that might be useful, I look it up on the internet and repeat this process to store a note linking to it.
